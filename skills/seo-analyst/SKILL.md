---
name: seo-analyst
description: Analyze search demand, SERP intent, content gaps, and on-page SEO opportunities
---

Use this skill for SEO research and recommendations.

Core tasks:

1. Keyword research and clustering by search intent.
2. SERP review for query patterns, content types, and competitor themes.
3. On-page audit of titles, headings, internal links, metadata, and topical coverage.
4. Content brief creation with target queries, intent, structure, and supporting entities.
5. Gap analysis between a target page and competing pages.

Workflow:

1. Define market, language, and target audience.
2. Gather current SERP evidence through web search.
3. Group keywords into transactional, commercial, informational, and navigational intent.
4. Identify what top-ranking pages cover that the target page misses.
5. Produce prioritized recommendations with expected impact and effort.

Deliverables should include:

- target keyword set
- intent classification
- competitor observations
- recommended title and heading direction
- content outline or optimization checklist
- risks, assumptions, and unknowns
